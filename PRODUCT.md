## Overview / Обзор

SOH is a desktop-first operations hub for managing Steam workflows at scale. It combines account management, proxy assignment, inventory views, bulk trade/sell/buy flows, Steam Guard confirmations, and Telegram-based licensing.

SOH — desktop-приложение для массовой работы со Steam-сценариями. В одном интерфейсе собраны аккаунты, прокси, инвентарь, bulk trade/sell/buy, Steam Guard подтверждения и лицензирование через Telegram.

---

## Features / Возможности

- **Accounts / Аккаунты**: import, groups, tags, archive mode, quick account actions.
- **Proxies / Прокси**: proxy assignment, availability checks, protocol detection, proxy-lane aware workflows.
- **Inventory / Инвентарь**: account inventory views, sync, value summaries, balances, and preparation for bulk flows.
- **Bulk operations / Массовые операции**: trade, sell, buy, and cancel listings with per-account progress.
- **Steam Guard**: maFile support, trade/market confirmations, and session-aware operations.
- **Licensing / Лицензии**: Free activation, subscription purchase, CryptoBot payment, and license key delivery through Telegram.
